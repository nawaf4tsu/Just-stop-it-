Practical Strategies

How to Stay Strong

01

Identify Your Triggers

Boredom, loneliness, stress — know what leads you there. Awareness is the first defense.

02

Exercise Daily

Physical activity burns excess energy, boosts mood naturally, and redirects urges into strength.

03

Lower Screen Time

Avoid idle phone use, especially at night. Use app blockers and keep your phone out of the bedroom.

04

Pray & Reflect

Spiritual practice strengthens your resolve. Fasting, dhikr, and salah build real inner discipline.

05

Stay Busy & Purposeful

A focused, goal-driven mind has no room for bad habits. Build something. Learn something. Create.

06

Accountability

Tell a trusted friend or join a community. Accountability doubles your chances of success.
